Handling of date time offsets

Start date and time and end date and time are given with a specific offset. The Client Service will respect different date time offsets

e.g. the date 2020-12-11 14:30:00+01:00 is include in the static interval specification with a lower boundary of 2020-12-11 13:00:00+00:00 and an upper boundary of 2020-12-11 12:00:00-02:00
